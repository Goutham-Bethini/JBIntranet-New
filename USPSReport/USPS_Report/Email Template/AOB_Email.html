Welcome to J&B Medical,

 

We are looking forward to providing you with excellent products and outstanding customer service!

 

Please log on to www.jandbportal.com and select Register to set up your online access.

 

The portal will provide you access to send J&B communications, check the status of your order, obtain tracking information on your shipped orders, pay out of pocket expenses, and more! 

 

Once your online account has been established please review and complete the Assignment of Benefits Form by selecting Forms. 

 

Please note that we will not be able to send you supplies until this form has been completed. 

 

Because your satisfaction is important to us, we want to share some important information with you:

 

Reordering made easy!

 

Your plan may not allow automatic reorders and may require that you confirm your future orders before they are shipped.  You can reorder your supplies 7 days before your next order is due to ship.  To reorder your supplies log on to www.jandbportal.com and select Orders.  You can sign up for text alerts by selecting My Account and Editing your Text Alert preference or confirm through our automated phone system (Victor) who will proactively contact you 7 days before your order is due to ship. 

 

Account Security

 

We pay close attention to the security of your account.  We never share information with anyone who cannot provide us with the required Protected Health Information (PHI) to unlock your account.  Please make sure you have alerted us of any authorized contacts that we have permission to speak with and ensure they have your insurance identification number(s), date of birth, address and last 4 digits of your Social Security number (SSN). 

 

Account Changes

 

It is important that you contact us at least 10 days before your next scheduled shipment if there are any changed with your account or order information. Failure to do so may result in a delay of supplies and account discrepancies. 

 

Returns and Exchanges

 

J&B adheres to a very strict 10 day return policy. It is important that you open all deliveries from J&B immediately and report any issues. J&B cannot guarantee return or exchange once 10 days have passed.  

 

Off Label

 

If your prescriber orders you a product to be used for a condition or use other than which has been approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA), additional paperwork may be required.  

 

Out of Pocket Expenses

 

While we do our best to provide you with the most accurate out of pocket expense information before your order ships it is the patients responsibility to know the expenses set forth by their health plan. J&B attempts to collect all estimated expenses up front, before the order ships, however, if your health plan applies additional cost share to your order you will receive a statement in the mail. Balances must be resolved within 30 days to continue receiving ongoing shipments. Our Billing department is happy to help you with any questions you many have regarding your statements, please contact them at (800) 737-0045 extension 166.
